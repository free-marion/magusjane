Art Style Options

Dark Pixel Art

RECOMMENDED

16×16 or 32×32 sprites with a restricted palette. Gritty, readable, very mobile-friendly. Closest to the current aesthetic. Assets are available now.

Hand-Drawn / Valiant Hearts

ASPIRATIONAL

Illustrated, painterly. Warmer and more emotional — the Valiant Hearts approach. Would require custom illustration or a very specific artist hire.

Silhouette / Limbo Style

ALTERNATIVE

Pure black silhouettes, single fog colour, minimal palette. Extremely atmospheric. Would need almost zero external art — mostly achievable with improved programmatic drawing.

Visual Reference — Target Feel

The closest game reference is Valiant Hearts: The Great War (Ubisoft, 2014) — hand-drawn, emotionally rich WWI art. We're going darker and more gritty (less cartoon) but the tone and palette approach is similar. The silhouette style of Limbo is also relevant for the forest sections.
